About Me

Visionary. Father. Creator.

I'm a single father and aspiring entrepreneur, raising my last 3 kids. I'm also a proud grandfather of 3 boys who my oldest son and his wife have blessed me with.

Late Career

I've started my career very late in life due to choices I made in my younger years. Though some of those choices may not have been the best, I now embrace and appreciate where they have landed me. I could have been somewhere living high off the hog, with no appreciation for life and just not caring about anything. Worst case, I could have been 6 feet deep by now, but instead, I've managed to change the trajectory, and here I am today.

What Have I Accomplished?

It is June 19, 2025 and I am now 55 years old. Over the years, I have managed to go back to college, earn a couple degrees in internet marketing and create a web design and digital marketing company. It's a small company but something I am passionate about. The business allows me to combine my creativity and entrepreneurial spirit into something that I would do for free if I had to. While success and monetary gains are an added bonus, it is not what drives me. The creative outlet and the joy of seeing happy customers are priceless.
